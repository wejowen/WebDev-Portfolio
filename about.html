About me

Hi, I'm William Owen, a professional with a strong passion for leveraging technology to solve problems and support people in their everyday lives. Currently, I work as a Law Clerk at Anvil Legal, where I have expanded my expertise in document management, compliance, and IT support.

My journey into technology started with my curiosity for how systems work. Over time, this curiosity evolved into practical skills, including providing technical support, troubleshooting operating systems, and managing virtual environments. Whether it's resolving a network issue or helping colleagues navigate technical challenges, I find fulfillment in making technology more accessible.

Beyond my work, I'm enhancing my skills through formal education at North Metropolitan TAFE, where I've explored programming, software development, and mock IT support systems. I believe that continual learning is the key to growth, and I'm excited about the possibilities of advancing my knowledge in programming and other related fields.

Outside of the professional realm, I enjoy tackling puzzles, whether they're digital or analog, and find inspiration in creative problem solving. I have a passion for technology, which drives me to build and customize computers and experiment with various Linux distributions. For me, technology is a practical tool that enhances efficiency and helps solve everyday challenges.
